Scholarite : A Whole Scholarship Portal

Built to provide transparency and efficiency to the disbursement of financial aid to students of an Institute. Scholarite is an integrated approach to making all the scholarships available to students on a common platform. This will not only make the discoverability of the scholarships easier, but also make the whole process transparent and fair.
